Our mission

NASA Robotic Mining Competition

NASA's annual Robot Mining Competition that is held at the Kennedy Space Center. The competition challenges colleges around the country to design and build an autonomous robot that can navigate an arena that simulates the surface of the Moon.

The objective of the robot is to traverse the arena, mine icy regolith simulant, and then return the collected substance to the collection bin.
